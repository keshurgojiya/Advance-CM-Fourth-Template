Airbnb has announced a significant change that will impact all software-connected hosts using platforms like AdvanceCM/Tokeet.

What's Changing?

Effective October 27, 2025, Airbnb will move all
software-connected hosts from the current split-fee model to a
single fee model. Under this model:

  • Airbnb will deduct a 15.5% service fee from your pricing to calculate your payout.
  • Guests will no longer see a separate Airbnb service fee during checkout.

This change is intended to simplify pricing and help hosts set competitive rates with a clearer view of their expected payouts.

What You Should Know

Airbnb advises reviewing your nightly rates to maintain your earnings, since the fee will now be taken from your price, resulting in lower earnings per night if left unchanged.
